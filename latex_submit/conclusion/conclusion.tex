\chapter{Conclusion}
In summary, the proposed rotating tilted platform design addresses most of the drawbacks of the current DIY solutions presented at \cref{sec:diy_solutions}: 
scanning time is relatively fast thanks to \gls{lidar} type, the platform is rigid, simple in its design, and produces a more uniform point cloud, granted that a suitable tilt angle is used. 

The introduction of a gearbox represents a key innovation, as it provides the user with control over the trade-off between speed, point cloud density, and environmental coverage. 
Thus, the resulting platform achieves the best overall compromise among DIY solutions while remaining affordable, as all the parts can be 3D-printed due to their simple design. 
This makes such a platform ideal for an amateur use case, which is what this project meant to achieve.
