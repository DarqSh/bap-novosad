The main drawbacks of the existing DIY solutions for a cheap \gls{lidar} scanner were presented in \cref{sec:diy_solutions}.

Then, the design of a rotating tilted platform was presented in \cref{sec:proposed_design} which solved all but one problem -- insufficient resolution.

Introducing a gearbox to our design is a viable solution which will not only lets us scan with higher resolution, 
but also choose if the scanning speed or point cloud coverage is more important in a certain scenario.
